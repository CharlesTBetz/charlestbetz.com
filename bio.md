# Charles T. Betz — Composer

Charles T. Betz is a Twin Cities-based composer and lyricist working in choral music and musical theater. His works have been performed by choirs and ensembles across the country, including First Presbyterian Church in Evanston, IL and Crescendo Community Chorus in Minneapolis.

In 2024, Charles was admitted to the [Nautilus Composer-Librettist Studio](https://nautilus-genome-project.squarespace.com/2024-mn-cl-studio), an intensive workshop for developing new musical theater works. He is a participant in the [New Musicals Inc.](http://nmi.org) program and a member of the [New Musical Theatre Exchange](/shows/).

His current major project is **Five Minutes On The Low Road**, a musical about a woman who has a near-death experience and the consequences that follow. He is also developing choral works exploring themes of nature, mortality, and the human spirit.

Charles holds a commitment to the craft of musical storytelling, drawing inspiration from both the classical choral tradition and contemporary musical theater.
