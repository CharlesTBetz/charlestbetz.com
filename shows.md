## Five Minutes On The Low Road

A musical about a woman who has a near-death experience, and the consequences that follow. Currently in development with book writer [Josiah Thomas Turner](https://newplayexchange.org/users/20167/josiah-thomas-turner), who joined the project in early 2025 after we met at the Nautilus Composer-Librettist Studio.

Music & Lyrics by Charles T. Betz. Book by Josiah Thomas Turner & Charles T. Betz.

**Status:** Act I music complete. Book in development.
